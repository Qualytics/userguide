# Assign a Datastore Group

This guide walks you through the steps to assign a datastore to an existing group.

!!! note
    You need **Editor** permission on the datastore to assign it to a group.

## Steps

**Step 1**: In the tree view, hover over the datastore you want to assign. The **Assign to group :material-bookmark-box-outline:** button will appear.

![step-1-assign-icon](../../../../assets/source-datastores/grouping/managing-groups/assign-a-datastore/step-1-assign-icon.png)

**Step 2**: Click the **Assign to group :material-bookmark-box-outline:** button. A dropdown will appear with the list of available groups.

![step-2-assign-dropdown](../../../../assets/source-datastores/grouping/managing-groups/assign-a-datastore/step-2-assign-dropdown.png)

**Step 3**: Select the group you want to assign the datastore to.

![step-3-datastore-assigned](../../../../assets/source-datastores/grouping/managing-groups/assign-a-datastore/step-3-datastore-assigned.png)

**Step 4**: The datastore will immediately move under the selected group in the tree view, and the group icon will be displayed next to the datastore.

![step-4-datastore-in-group](../../../../assets/source-datastores/grouping/managing-groups/assign-a-datastore/step-4-datastore-in-group.png)

!!! tip
    You can also **create a new group** directly from the assign menu by clicking the **Create** button, if you have the Manager role.
