# Assign Tag

Assigning tags to your Datastore helps you to identify and categorize your datastore easily. Tags serve as labels that categorize and identify various data sets, enhancing efficiency and organization. By highlighting checks and anomalies, tags make it easier to monitor data quality. They also allow you to list file patterns and assign quality scores, enabling quick identification and resolution of issues.

## Steps

**Step 1**: Select the datastore from the tree view on the left side. In the footer panel, locate the **Assign tags to this datastore** section.

**Step 2**: Click on the **Assign tags to this datastore** section to expand the tag selector.

![step-2-assign-tags-button](../../assets/source-datastores/tags/assign-tags/step-2-assign-tags-button.png)

**Step 3**: A dropdown will appear with available tags. Select the tags you want to assign to your datastore.

![step-3-select-tags](../../assets/source-datastores/tags/assign-tags/step-3-select-tags.png)

**Step 4**: Once assigned, the tags will be instantly labeled on your source datastore, and all related records will be updated.

![step-4-tags-assigned](../../assets/source-datastores/tags/assign-tags/step-4-tags-assigned.png)
