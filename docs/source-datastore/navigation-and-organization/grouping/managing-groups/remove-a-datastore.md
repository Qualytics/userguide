# Remove a Datastore from a Group

This guide walks you through the steps to remove a datastore from its current group.

!!! note
    You need **Editor** permission on the datastore to remove it from a group.

## Steps

**Step 1**: In the tree view, hover over the datastore you want to remove from its group. An **assign menu** icon will appear.

![assign-icon](../../../../assets/source-datastores/navigation-and-organization/grouping/managing-groups/remove-a-datastore/assign-icon.png)

**Step 2**: Click the assign menu icon. A dropdown will appear showing the currently assigned group.

![assign-dropdown](../../../../assets/source-datastores/navigation-and-organization/grouping/managing-groups/remove-a-datastore/assign-dropdown.png)

**Step 3**: Click the **close icon** (x) next to the currently selected group.

![remove-group](../../../../assets/source-datastores/navigation-and-organization/grouping/managing-groups/remove-a-datastore/remove-group.png)

**Step 4**: The datastore will move to the **Ungrouped** section of the tree view.

![datastore-ungrouped](../../../../assets/source-datastores/navigation-and-organization/grouping/managing-groups/remove-a-datastore/datastore-ungrouped.png)
