# ServiceNow

The **ServiceNow** integration is available in the **Qualytics** platform for tracking and managing data quality issues through ServiceNow incidents. It allows teams to route data quality issues detected in **Qualytics** into their existing **ServiceNow** workflows for investigation and resolution.
